About Us

Who We Are?

At J&A, we are a lifestyle destination dedicated to providing comfort, relaxation, and great food all in one place. We strive to create a warm and welcoming space where guests can unwind, enjoy delicious meals, and feel right at home.

Our services include a peaceful spa for relaxation and self-care, a cozy café perfect for coffee and light snacks, a vibrant food court offering a variety of flavorful dishes, and comfortable residences for both short and long stays.

Whether you’re here to relax, dine, or find a cozy place to stay, J&A is committed to making your experience enjoyable and memorable.

Brief History of J&A

February 4, 2023 Grand Opening of J&A Foodcourt Residence

J&A Restaurant & Residence opened its doors on February 4, 2023 , with the dream of creating a place where people can relax, enjoy good food, and feel at home. From the start, our goal has been to offer a space where guests can eat, stay, and unwind—all in one spot.

April 2, 2024 Grand Opening of J&A Cafe

J&A was created to be a place where people can relax, enjoy good food, and feel at home. A big part of our journey was the opening of J&A Café on April 2, 2024 . On that day, we welcomed coffee lovers and food enthusiasts to a cozy spot where they could enjoy freshly made coffee, tasty pastries, and light snacks.
